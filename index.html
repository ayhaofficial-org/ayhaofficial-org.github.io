Welcome to AYHA

African Youths Humanitarian Association (AYHA) is a youths-led organization focused on empowering young people across Africa and Africans in the diaspora through education, leadership development, and community support initiatives. We promote Love, Peace And Unity amongst Africans Worldwide.

Our Mission

To support African youths and vulnerable communities with resources, training, and opportunities to grow and thrive, while promoting humanitarian values and youths participation.

Our Focus Areas

  • Youth empowerment and leadership
  • Education and skills development
  • Community outreach and humanitarian support
  • Advocacy for youths rights and inclusion
