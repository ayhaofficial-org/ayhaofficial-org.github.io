Welcome to AYHA

African Youths Humanitarian Association (AYHA) is a youth-led humanitarian organization focused on empowering African youths and supporting vulnerable communities through education, leadership development, advocacy and practical humanitarian action.

Our Mission

To support African youths and vulnerable communities with resources, training and opportunities to grow and thrive, while promoting humanitarian values and youths participation across Africa.

Our Focus Areas

  • Youths empowerment and leadership
  • Education and skills development
  • Community outreach and humanitarian support
  • Advocacy for youths rights and inclusion
