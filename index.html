Welcome to AYHA

African Youths Humanitarian Association (AYHA) is a youth-led humanitarian organization dedicated to empowering African youths and assisting vulnerable communities across Africa.

Our Mission

To support African youths with education, leadership training, community development, and humanitarian assistance across the continent.

  • Youths empowerment & leadership
  • Education & skills development
  • Humanitarian & community outreach
  • Advocacy for youths rights & inclusion
